# 🔥 1. YES / NO QUESTIONS (Most Common)

### Do / Does

* Do you like it? → **D’ya like it?**
* Do you need help? → **D’ya need help?**
* Does he work here? → **Does he work here?** (less reduction)

---

### Did

* Did you eat? → **Didja eat?**
* Did you finish? → **Didja finish?**
* Did you call him? → **Didja call ’im?**

---

### Are / Is / Am

* Are you ready? → **Are ya ready?**
* Are you coming? → **Are ya coming?**
* Is he here? → **Is he here?** (clear usually)

---

### Can / Could

* Can you help me? → **Can ya help me?**
* Can you do it? → **Can ya do it?**
* Could you wait? → **Couldja wait?**

---

### Will / Would

* Will you come? → **Will ya come?**
* Would you like it? → **Wouldja like it?**

---

# 🔥 2. WH- QUESTIONS (VERY IMPORTANT)

### What

* What are you doing? → **Whatcha doing?**
* What do you want? → **Whaddya want?**
* What did you say? → **What’d ya say?**
* What is that? → **What’s that?**

---

### Where

* Where are you going? → **Where ya going?**
* Where do you live? → **Where d’ya live?**
* Where did you go? → **Where’d ya go?**

---

### Why

* Why are you late? → **Why ya late?**
* Why did you do that? → **Why’d ya do that?**

---

### When

* When are you coming? → **When ya coming?**
* When did you start? → **When’d ya start?**

---

### Who

* Who are you talking to? → **Who’re ya talking to?**
* Who did you meet? → **Who’d ya meet?**

---

### How

* How are you? → **How are ya?**
* How do you know? → **How d’ya know?**
* How did you do that? → **How’d ya do that?**

---

# 🔥 3. “GOING TO / WANT TO” QUESTIONS

* Are you going to go? → **Are ya gonna go?**
* Do you want to eat? → **D’ya wanna eat?**
* Are you going to work? → **Are ya gonna work?**

---

# 🔥 4. TAG QUESTIONS (VERY COMMON IN REAL TALK)

* You’re coming, right?
* You like it, don’t you?
* He’s here, isn’t he?
* We can go, can’t we?
* You finished, didn’t you?

👉 Fast speech:

* **You’re coming, right?**
* **Nice, isn’t it?**

---

# 🔥 5. SHORT CASUAL QUESTIONS

These are everywhere in daily conversation:

* You coming?
* You okay?
* You good?
* All set?
* Ready?
* Got it?
* Need help?
* Want some?
* Coming with us?
* Finished?

---

# 🔥 6. INDIRECT QUESTIONS (Polite / Formal)

* Can you tell me where he is?
* Do you know what time it is?
* Could you tell me how this works?
* Do you know why he left?

👉 Spoken fast:

* **D’ya know where he is?**
* **Can ya tell me what time it is?**

---

# 🔥 7. NEGATIVE QUESTIONS (IMPORTANT FOR IELTS)

* Don’t you like it?
* Didn’t you see that?
* Aren’t you coming?
* Can’t you do it?

👉 Spoken:

* **Don’tcha like it?**
* **Didn’tcha see that?**

---

# 🔥 8. REAL DAILY COMBO QUESTIONS (🔥 GOLD)

These are exactly how natives speak:

* **Whatcha gonna do?**
* **D’ya wanna go?**
* **Where ya gonna stay?**
* **Why’d ya say that?**
* **How’d ya know that?**
* **You gonna come or what?**
* **Can ya give me a hand?**
* **Didja get my message?**
* **Whaddya mean?**
* **What’re ya talking about?**

---

# 🎯 What you should focus on (honest advice)

Top priority:

1. **D’ya / Didja / Whatcha / Whaddya**
2. **Where ya / Why’d ya / How’d ya**
3. **Gonna / Wanna in questions**
4. Short questions (You coming?)
